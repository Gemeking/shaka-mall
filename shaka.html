About Shaka Mall

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Ashewa Meda, Oromia Region, Shaka Mall stands as a beacon of modern retail excellence blended seamlessly with Ethiopia's rich cultural heritage. Opened in recent years, our mall has quickly become a go-to destination for locals and visitors alike, offering a diverse range of shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

Our architecture draws inspiration from traditional Ethiopian motifs, featuring intricate patterns and warm color palettes that reflect the Oromia region's artistic legacy. With over 50 stores spanning multiple floors, Shaka Mall provides everything from high-end fashion to everyday essentials, all under one roof.

Committed to community development, we host regular cultural events, workshops, and markets that celebrate local artisans and entrepreneurs. Whether you're shopping for the latest gadgets or savoring authentic Ethiopian cuisine, Shaka Mall promises an unforgettable experience.
